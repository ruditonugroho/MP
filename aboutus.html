Welcome to Coastal County FC — Where Passion Meets the Pitch

Founded with a vision to unite our community through the beautiful game, Coastal County FC is more than just a football club — we are a movement, a family, and a source of pride for our region. Nestled along the coast, our club reflects the strength, resilience, and unity of our people.

From grassroots development to competitive league play, we are committed to nurturing talent, promoting sportsmanship, and inspiring the next generation of footballers. Every match we play is driven by dedication, teamwork, and a deep love for the game.

At Coastal County FC, we believe that football has the power to bring people together — across neighborhoods, generations, and backgrounds. Whether you’re a player, supporter, or partner, you are part of our journey.

Our mission is simple: To grow the game, serve the community, and chase excellence on and off the field.

Join us. Feel the energy. Be part of the Coastal County FC legacy.
